I don’t know if I have anything of real value to add here 😅 The term “meditation society” is more widely used by most groups and reknown teachers out there, should you do a search for it, and Lukas gives a good reason to not pick that one as well.

If we are looking at the origination of the word Sangha that held that space in the past, we find translations like “association”, “assembly”, “company” or “community”. And probably there are many more. But some traditions don’t like it being used for lay people, which I didn’t know before.

So yeah, you’ll find an established concepts behind each of those terms, but whichever one you choose will be the right one since it’s mostly just a label 😄 So I’d support any term you guys want to go with 🗳️
